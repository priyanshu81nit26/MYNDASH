# 💎 MYNDASH PRO — features so good that free feels like spectating

Design law for every item here: **paying never buys wins** (XP and ratings stay
pure) — Pro buys *insight, identity, access, and time*. The feeling we're
engineering: a free user watches a Pro user's profile and thinks *"okay, I need
that."*

---

## 1. 🤖 COACH MYNDASH — your personal AI trainer (the killer feature)

Not stats. A **coach with a personality** that watches every game you play:

- **Blunder Autopsy** — after any chess game, replay with coach arrows: "move 14 — you hung the fork. Here's the line you missed." Same for darts (release-timing heat chart) and cube (move-efficiency vs optimal).
- **Tilt Detector** — it notices you lost 3 in a row at 1 AM and literally locks in a "cooldown puzzle" instead of letting you burn rating. Users will screenshot this.
- **Weakness Radar** — "your accuracy drops 34% on probability questions under 10 seconds." Then it builds tomorrow's training plan around exactly that.
- **Pre-match scouting** — before an online duel: "@rival is 71% weaker on geometry — pick it."

*Why Pro feels it:* free users get one coach insight per week as a taste. Pros live with a full-time cornerman.

## 2. 🎞 CINEMA REPLAYS + AUTO-HIGHLIGHTS

Every Pro match is recorded as a replay (moves + timestamps, kilobytes). One tap
renders a **shareable highlight reel card**: "Checkmate in 19 · 94% accuracy ·
clutch dart 98 under pressure." Your best moment of the week auto-compiles into
a **Weekly Wrapped** story. Free users can *watch* Pro replays — that's the ad.

## 3. 🧬 LIVING PROFILE — identity flex pack

- **Animated Mind DNA** card (pulses with your last 7 days of form)
- **Entrance animations** in arenas — your nameplate slams in with your title
- **Signature board sets**: neon chess boards, gold dartboards, chrome cubes, custom confetti & victory sounds
- **Prestige frames** that evolve with win streaks (bronze ember → violet plasma)
- A **/mynd.gg/username** public page — your card, form, trophies, live

## 4. 🥷 SHADOW LEAGUE — Pro-only Sunday night league

Invisible until you're in it. 20:00 Sunday, 8-player brackets seeded by rating,
**double rating stakes**, spectate-enabled, winner gets an animated belt on
their profile for the week + a real store voucher. The FOMO engine: results are
public, entry is Pro.

## 5. 🔮 GHOST OF YOURSELF

Race the ghost of your own best runs (daily-5 time, cube solve, dart total) —
your past self's progress bar breathing down your neck is the single most
addictive single-player mechanic ever shipped. Pros also get **rival ghosts**:
challenge any leaderboard player's recorded run asynchronously.

## 6. 🛰 UNLIMITED EVERYTHING + FAST LANE

- Unlimited real-chess engine games, journey retries, IQ sets & cube races (free tier: daily caps)
- **Priority matchmaking** — Pros match first when queues are thin
- **2× arena tickets** weekly + free Gold-arena hosting with custom rules (question mix, time controls, up to 128 seats)
- Rematch instantly with the same seed. Free users wait for a new room.

## 7. 📊 DEEP STATS VAULT

Percentile curves vs the whole planet, per-category speed distributions,
rating graphs with event annotations ("joined squad NEON here"), opening
repertoire tracker for chess, per-ring accuracy for darts, cube split-times
(cross/F2L-style phase timing). Exportable. Nerd heaven — and nerds pay.

## 8. 🎓 MASTERCLASS DROPS

Monthly in-app interactive courses in the exact MYNDASH question engine: "Mental
math like a trader," "Mate-in-two patterns," "4×4 parity for humans." Solve
along inside the lesson. One free lesson per course as the hook.

## 9. 🧊 COLD-STREAK INSURANCE

Twice a month, a Pro can **freeze one loss** (rating protected) within 60
seconds of the result — the "phone rang mid-duel" eraser. Capped so integrity
holds, priceless for feel. (Never usable in Shadow League or contests.)

## 10. 👑 FOUNDER'S TABLE (Ultra)

Top Ultra tier: vote on the roadmap monthly, name appears in the app's
credits screen, early access lane (2 weeks), a yearly physical merch drop,
and one **custom bot persona** trained on your own play style that friends
can challenge — "beat the robo-you."
