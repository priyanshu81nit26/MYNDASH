# Retired PRO plan

MYNDASH PRO and all paid subscription surfaces were retired on 19 July 2026.

- AI Trainer is available to every player.
- Arena hosting limits no longer depend on membership.
- No subscription or payment checkout is offered in v1.
- The Store is preview-only and coins are earned through gameplay.

This filename remains only to prevent old documentation links from breaking.
Future paid functionality must be designed as a new v2 system; do not restore
the retired Razorpay implementation. See `docs/payments-v1-retired.md`.
